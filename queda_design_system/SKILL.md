## Quick reference

- **queda is** a dark, mobile-first social app for spontaneous plans (24–72h horizon).
- **Aesthetic:** near-black canvas, cream ink, one lime accent. No gradients on surfaces, no shadows, no imagery. Speed over motion.
- **Type:** Syne 800 (display) + DM Sans (body), loaded from Google Fonts CDN.
- **Radii:** 14px cards, 10px buttons, 999 pills.
- **Wordmark:** `queda` lowercase, trailing period in `#CDFF6C` — always.
- **Voice:** blunt, action-verb, second-person, sentence case.

See `README.md` for full content, visual, and iconography guidelines.
See `colors_and_type.css` for tokens.
